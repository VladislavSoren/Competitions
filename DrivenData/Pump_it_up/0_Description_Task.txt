Goal: 
To predict the operating condition of a waterpoint for each record in the dataset.

Distribution of Labels:
	functional 				- the waterpoint is operational and there are no repairs needed
	functional needs repair - the waterpoint is operational, but needs repairs
	non functional 			- the waterpoint is not operational

Суть:
Нужно решить задачу класиификации, т.е. по данным, которые
характеризуют водоколонки в Танзании (исходный DataSet)
предсказать её состояние:
	functional, 				
	functional needs repair, 
	non functional.
